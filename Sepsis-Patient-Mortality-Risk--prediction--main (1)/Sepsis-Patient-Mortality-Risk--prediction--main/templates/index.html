Process

How It Works

Enter patient vitals, lab values, and clinical context. The model returns a calibrated mortality probability in seconds.

1

Enter Patient Data

Input demographics, SOFA score, vital signs (min/max/mean), lab results, comorbidities, and antibiotic class.

2

Model Inference

Data is scaled using the training preprocessor, then passed to the tuned XGBoost classifier for probability estimation.

3

Risk Stratification

The predicted probability is compared against the F2-optimised threshold and classified as Low / Moderate / High / Critical.
